Our Mission

We aim to create a hub of arts, culture, and design in Musanze to empower young people with creative opportunities. Many youths have talent but lack spaces and support to grow. This hub will help them learn, collaborate, and showcase their work while connecting art with tourism and business. Our goal is to turn creativity into opportunity and drive community development.
